PAYMENT PROTECTION
How do I make my online payment secure?

Make sure that both Wi-Fi connection and the website URL that you are using are secure. Never share personal or financial data over a public Wi-Fi connection. If you must shop over public Wi-Fi, use a VPN (virtual private network) to add security to the transaction.

What do you do with unauthorized transactions?

Report a suspicious charge or debit immediately. Contact your bank or cart provider immediately if you suspect an unauthorized debit or charge. If a thief charges items to you account, you should cancel the card and have it replaced before more transactions come through. Contact GetReadyPH customer service to verify and freeze the transactions on your account.

What to do if an unauthorized person is requesting to receive the payment outside GRPH website or application?

Payments made by customers to persons who were not given authority by GRPH, by law or by the courts are not valid. Always make sure to complete your transaction via GRPH website or application. Beware of online scammers.
