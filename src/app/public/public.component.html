Welcome to Get Ready! The first fully integrated one-stop virtual portal in the Philippines dedicated to Safety and Emergency Preparedness Needs. It's an online resource center, an online help reference center and an e-shopping site for your essential preparedness needs, all rolled into one!

The world is no longer a safe place. Pandemics, natural disasters, man induced terrors, accidents are constantly making headlines all over the world. They can happen anytime, anywhere. Here at GetReadyPH, we strive to provide you with a one-stop online resource center and e-marketplace where you can learn and find out what to do, where to seek help and what you need to have to be better prepared and equipped for the unknown.
