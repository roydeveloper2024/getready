Frequently Asked Questions

What is Get Ready PH?

Get Ready Ph is your go-to, one stop e-commerce site for your safety, emergency, and disaster preparedness supplies needs.

Do I need to have an account for me to purchase an item in your site?

It is not mandatory but is strongly encouraged. You may still purchase using a guest account.

What is the area of coverage for deliveries?

We can deliver anywhere in the Philippines through third-party couriers. Delivery is charge to the customer. Delivery outside the Philippines is not yet available.

How soon can I get the product I ordered?

Please allow 5 to 7 business days order processing. Once your payment is validated, we will immediately schedule the delivery the following business day.

Do your items come with warranty?

Most of the items comes with different warranties. You will see the warranty on the product details.

How do I return defective items?

All products listed on our site goes through a quality control process. In case of defects, please immediately inform us through e-mail or chat and we will arrange pick-up for assessment and or replacement.

We accept the following Modes of Payments:
  • GCash
  • Online Transfer
  • Over-The-Counter or OTC Bank Deposit
  • Cash On Delivery (Orders P4,000. 00 and below)
