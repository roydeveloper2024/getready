Businesses and employers play a key role in preventing and slowing the spread of the SARS-CoV-2 within the company. BBI ensures the safety of each employee

Workplace

In BBI, we have implemented a workplace worry-free environment.

As you enter the workplace, everything is being checked from your body temperature, wearing the right face mask, asking each employees to sanitize their hands.

Air purifier, with UV light, is placed in each room. We ensure that we have a good ventilation in every corner of the office.

And as part of the prevention, we allotted the right number of tables and chairs for each room to observe social distancing in each room. And everything is being sanitized every time just to make sure the safety of everyone.

BBI is well prepared as we have an isolation room for those employees showing symptoms and our nurse will take care of them.

Travel Support

BBI allotted shuttle service for all the employees for them not to get exposed. All employees are being shuttled going to the office and from going back home.

Before employees go on board, body temperature is also being checked, sanitizes their hands and ensuring that they have face mask.

All shuttle services are equipped of air purifiers with UV light
