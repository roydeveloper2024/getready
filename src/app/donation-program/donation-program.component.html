HOW OUR DONATION PROGRAM WORKS

This special program is created to allow everyone to help one another, VOLUNTARILY! Individuals, families or organizations that cannot afford to buy but need help to procure emergency life saving tools and equipment can submit their petition and appeal here. Donors who are willing to help but sometimes do not know how to help, where to help, or whom to help can now view the list of those Needing Help here and decide which petitioner they can help. Not only that, Donors can also procure items for others not in the list but to a specific friend or an organization of your own choice just by filling up the form, completing the order process and typing in their details and we will have the items delivered to them! It's an Easy, No Hassle Way to Help Each Other Save Lives!
